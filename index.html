About our Company

We are a small dedicated team, trying to solve the problems of travel industry

B2B Platform

We aim at creating the 1st fully remote & integrated marketplace for creating corporate connection among service providers in the travel industry. Tour Operators, Travel Agents, Hotels, Airlines, Car Rentals, & Bus Service provider can list their services over B2B platform & can connect with each other to create the best travel deals for travelers. This platform enables better networking among corporates of travel industry leading to better business deals & growth for businesses.

B2C Platform

We aim at providing the best travel deals to travelers through the B2C platform. Travelers can book individual travel services like Hotels, Flight, Bus service etc. or they can book a full travel package with the registered travel agents & tour operators on our platform. Travelers can also create unique experiences by specifying their customized needs over B2C platform, the unique experience would be created by the service providers by creating the customized service for travelers by connecting with each other over our B2B platform.

How It Works

We are a team of dedicated creators working on solving the major issues pertaining in travel industry.

Step 1

Step 2
